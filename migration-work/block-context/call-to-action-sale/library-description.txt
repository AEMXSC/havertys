Call to Action - Sale variant

A promotional call-to-action block designed for sale/event announcements with background imagery.
Uses a carousel structure (single or multi-slide) where each slide contains:
- Column 1: Background image (displayed as full-bleed behind content)
- Column 2: Content area with heading, subheading, and CTA button(s)

Purpose: Bold sale promotional banners with dark overlay on background images.
Content pattern: heading + subheading + 1 CTA button.
Visual: Dark background with white text overlay on a background image.
